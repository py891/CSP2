Project Overview

What we are investigating and why it matters.

Our CSP investigates how surface materials in built environments (roads, pavements, roofs, walls) affect heat absorption/retention and how that changes temperature patterns and energy demand (especially cooling). We connect physical mechanisms (radiation, conduction, thermal mass) with real-world environmental and societal impacts.

Main Question

How do surface materials influence temperature and energy demand in built environments?

Why it matters

Material choice affects urban temperature, comfort, biodiversity, and electricity used for cooling.
